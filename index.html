Factcheckr.io

Factcheckr.io supports checking of both widely accepted facts and potential fake news.

Examples of widely accepted facts

  • The sky is blue
  • The US population is 400 million
  • The Earth has a diameter of 100 miles
  • Chicago is the largest city in the US
  • The formula for water is H2O

Examples of potential fake news

  • Trump won the 2020 election
  • Wearing masks protects from Covid-19
  • Joe Biden supports Putin
  • Jeffrey Epstein didn't kill himself
  • Vaccines are effective at preventing Covid-19
